About Me

Hello! I'm Kurtney Agullano, a passionate front-end developer dedicated to crafting engaging and user-friendly web experiences. With a keen eye for design and a commitment to clean code, I strive to bring ideas to life on the web.

My journey in web development began with a fascination for how websites are built and how they function. Over the years, I've honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and various front-end frameworks to create responsive and accessible interfaces.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy exploring the latest trends in web design, contributing to open-source projects, and continuously learning new technologies to enhance my skill set.
